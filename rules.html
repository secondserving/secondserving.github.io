Second Serving Rules

By using Second Serving, you agree to the following rules. Breaking these rules might result in a blacklist from using Second Serving.

Rule One:

User-bots, Spamming, Alts and Macros

User-bots, macros, auto-typers and anything else that enables a user to use commands automatically is forbidden. Additionally, large amounts of spam is also not allowed. Lastly, the usage of alt accounts to bypass blacklists, boost statistics or gain unfair advantages such as (but not limited to) aditional entries in giveaways and lotteries are strictly prohibitted and will be punished severely

Rule Two:

The Use and Sharing of Exploits

The use and sharing of exploits is not allowed. Please report all exploits and bugs that you find in the Second Serving Support Server

Rule Three:

Inappropriate Naming

The use of inappropriate naming in Second Serving is not allowed. This includes (but is not limited to) shop names and guild names.

Rule Four:

Selling or Trading In-game Items

Selling or trading in-game items,such as, money and lootboxes, for real money, other bot currencies or items such as discord nitro is forbidden. Buying anything outside of our official shop will result in an instant blacklist.


Report rule breakers in our support server.
