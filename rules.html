Second Serving Rules

By using Second Serving, you agree to the following rules. Breaking these rules might result in a blacklist from using Second Serving.

Rule One:

User-bots, Spamming, Alts and Macros

User-bots, macros, auto-typers and anything else that enables a user to use commands automatically is forbidden. Additionally, large amounts of spam is also not allowed. Lastly, the usage of alt accounts to bypass blacklists, boost statistics or gain unfair advantages such as (but not limited to) aditional entries in giveaways and lotteries are strictly prohibitted and will be punished severely
